Context
UNCLOS is an acronym for „United Nations Convention on the Law of the Sea“ also called the „Law of the Sea Convention“ or the „Law of the Sea treaty“. This visualization shows the total 200 pages of this treaty, which was concluded inn 1982 and entered into force in 1994. According to the UN the treaty has 157 signatories.
The Convention also includes the Article 76; this article give states -under certain geological conditions- the rights to claim exclusive economic territory even beyond 200 nautical miles.
