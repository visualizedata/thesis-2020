How comes, that claims of some states of the global north contain such huge areas?
One reason is that they still profit from former colonized territories. Each circle encodes the total economic exploitable area of France (Exclusive Economic Zone plus potential claimed area in regard of the continental shelf). On the left related to the Mainland of France, on the right related to the Oversea Territories of France in the carribean, pacific and Indian Ocean. It shows that France profits from their Oversea Territory with almost 10 Million square kilometres. If all claims of France would be adopted, this number will increase up to 14 Million square kilometres.
